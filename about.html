HI, I'M SOPHIE!

I like languages -- of human, computer, and creative natures.

“Being creative” is vague, but I like how it's not too clear. It lets you use a little creativity of your own to figure out what it means.

It feels like I'm solving a puzzle (while sometimes a little carsick trying to match the pace of your thoughts) and seeing the rough edges of each jigsaw piece butter over into a smooth, ripply-soft water finish.
